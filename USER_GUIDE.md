# ShreeHariKripa Website User Guide

Welcome to the **ShreeHariKripa** platform! This user guide is designed to help you navigate and utilize all the features available on the website, whether you are a new customer, a returning shopper, or a site administrator.

---

## 🛍️ 1. Browsing and Shopping

### Home Page
The homepage is your starting point. Here you will find:
- **Banners & Promotions:** The latest deals and highlighted collections.
- **Featured Categories & Products:** Quick access to popular items.

### Shop Page
Navigate to the **Shop** page to see the full catalog of products.
- **Filters & Sorting:** Narrow down products by category, price, size, and other attributes.
- **Visual Search:** Looking for something specific? Use our visual search feature to find products that match an image you provide.

### Product Details
Click on any product to view its detailed page:
- View high-quality images and descriptions.
- Select your desired size, color, or variant.
- Check product availability and shipping information.
- Click **Add to Cart** or **Add to Wishlist**.

---

## 👤 2. User Accounts

### Registration & Login
To get the most out of the platform, you should create an account.
- **Register:** Click on the profile icon and select "Register". You will need to verify your email address after registration.
- **Login:** Access your account using your email and password. Use the "Forgot Password" link if you need to reset your credentials.

### Account Dashboard
Once logged in, you can access your Account Dashboard:
- **Profile:** Manage your personal information and addresses.
- **Wishlist:** View and manage items you've saved for later.
- **Security:** Update your password.

---

## 🛒 3. Checkout and Orders

### Cart
- Access your cart at any time by clicking the shopping bag icon.
- Review your selected items, adjust quantities, or remove products.
- Proceed to checkout when you're ready to complete your purchase.

### Order Tracking
- Navigate to the **Orders** section in your account.
- View a complete history of your past and current orders.
- Track the shipping status of your active orders in real-time.

---

## 🔄 4. Returns and Exchanges

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you need to make a return or exchange:
1. Go to the **Orders** section in your dashboard.
2. Select the eligible order and click on the **Return/Exchange** option.
3. Fill out the required details (reason for return, media evidence like images/videos if required).
4. Track your return request through the **Returns List** in your account.

*Please review our **Return Policy** page for detailed information on eligibility and timelines.*

---

## 👑 5. Administrator Guide (For Staff Only)

If you have an admin account, you have access to the backend management portal:
- **Admin Dashboard:** Get a bird's-eye view of total sales, active users, and key metrics.
- **Order Management:** Process new orders, update shipping statuses, and manage fulfillment.
- **Return Management:** 
  - Review incoming return requests and media evidence.
  - Approve or reject returns.
  - Update logistics statuses (e.g., "Pickup Scheduled", "Refund Processing").
- **Catalog Management:** Add, edit, or remove products, categories, and banners.

- **FAQs:** Find answers to commonly asked questions.
